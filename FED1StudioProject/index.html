About Us

We are Modern Missouri, a trusted company for over 30 years to teach Kansas City about the fundamentals of technology! Our course goes over computer hardware, operating systems, software, security, configuring workstations, and safety! After our course you will be able to branch out your education, our goal is to get you fundamentally prepared to be working with tech!

Why Choose Us

All of our instructors have experience working in the field so they know what they teach in-depth from a professional stand point.

1 on 1 time

Our student:teacher ratio is 10:1. With small classes, you will be able to have closer relationships with your classmates and most importantly your instructors.
